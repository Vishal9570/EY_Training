Chunking Analaysis:

The 256-token configuration produced the lowest latency (1.68s) but fragmented the document 
into 13 chunks, increasing the risk that related information would be split across multiple 
chunks. The 1024-token configuration reduced the number of chunks to 4 and achieved a 
latency of 1.88s, but larger chunks may include irrelevant information that can reduce 
retrieval precision. The 512-token configuration produced 7 chunks and the highest latency 
(2.40s), likely because each retrieved chunk contained more contextual information that 
required additional processing by the LLM. 

---Despite the higher latency, 512-token chunks 
provided the best balance between context preservation and retrieval effectiveness, 
making them the preferred choice for this financial RAG system.
